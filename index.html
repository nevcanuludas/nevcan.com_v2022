I started to breathing in underwater on my university years (2008) with the school's diving club. After my first dive, my instructor asked to me how I felt myself and I replied "I will become an instructor too". Then he smiled and said to me "You complete this course first, then you can be an instructor later".

Now, I am the first GUE instructor in Turkiye. On the other hand I realize worldwide education programs for protecting underwater and supporting exploration venture. Also I have been included in many different underwater exploration projects.

I am individually interested in technical deep diving, mixture gases, closed circuit rebreathers (CCR) and my actual passion cave diving.
